Flylady: Home Companion



Fly Lady is simple, encouraging, and designed to support the structure of housework systems that you already know and love, while still remaining flexible to your own customizations or your own way of working. In our app, we’ve brought together some of the best big ideas in home and household productivity, mixed them up, given them a good shake, and put them together again in a new way.
